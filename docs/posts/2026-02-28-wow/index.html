#futurewisyesterday

Can’t sleep?

Neither can I.

Some people count sheep. I build online shops under my own brand instead:

CTRLexist.com

Just my therapy disguised as e-commerce. Creative chaos with structure. Built between cost frames and coffee refills.

It terrifies me what technology allows already. A few hours at home, One unskilled curious beginner and an old mobile phone launched three years ago, plus a name, surname, email address and a PayPal account. That’s it.

Shock

Nothing limits us except imagination… and rising cost frames.

No apologies, but retailers juggling heavy warehouses and human power in the UK have to improvise to prove the meaning of existence.

After all… “We’re not in Kansas anymore.”

Custom pieces? I’m in.

If you have a vision but don’t know how to bring it to life, I can play around with it for you. For fun .
